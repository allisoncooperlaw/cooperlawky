I’ve seen clients who believed their will left everything to their trust — but the will didn’t name a trust. Their whole plan existed only in their imagination, and it cost them a few hundred dollars in wasted money. People shouldn’t throw money at a solution that might not even do what they want — especially when their goals weren’t complicated to begin with.

I’m not a replacement for your existing advisors. But because I understand what each of them does, I can build an estate plan that doesn’t contradict or ignore the rest of your financial life.

The difference it makes

Three credentials. One plan that actually works.

Your CPA sees the tax picture

Your CFP® sees the financial picture

When one person holds all three lenses, the gaps disappear. You get a plan where the legal structure, the tax strategy, and the financial picture all speak the same language — because they were designed together.
