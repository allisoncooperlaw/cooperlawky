Why timelines matter

A thorough estate plan typically takes 4–6 weeks from consultation to signed documents — intake, drafting, review, revisions, execution. Priority Planning compresses that entire process to 48–72 hours. Same careful work — focused timeline. That’s what the service is for, and why it’s priced as a premium.

Subject to attorney availability

Priority Planning is offered subject to Allison’s current availability and caseload. The 48–72 hour turnaround begins when all intake materials are received and availability is confirmed — not from the date of initial inquiry. If you have a hard deadline such as a surgery date or departure date, please state it clearly in your intake form so Allison can confirm she can meet your timeline before you engage. Cooper Law will always be upfront if a requested timeline cannot be accommodated.
