Services & Fees

Transparent, flat-fee estate planning

Attorney fees for estate planning are often more comparable to online services than people expect — especially when you factor in the cost of fixing documents that weren’t set up correctly in the first place. Every Cooper Law engagement includes document drafting and a written summary of recommended next steps. Asset titling, deed preparation, beneficiary designation changes, and coordination with other advisors are separate engagements — see add-on services below.

On timelines: Estimates depend on client responsiveness, document complexity, and current caseload. The clock starts when Cooper Law has received all completed intake materials — not from the date of initial inquiry. All timelines are subject to attorney availability. Clients who return intake materials promptly typically complete their plan at the shorter end of the range.

Document Delivery

Your choice on every service

📧

Electronic delivery

Secure PDF with annotated signing instructions. Callout boxes show exactly what to sign, witness, and notarize — and where. Print at home and execute. Fast and convenient for most clients.

📬

Mailed signing kit

Printed documents mailed with tabbed signing instructions and signing stickers. Best for clients who prefer physical documents. Standard shipping included. Allow 3–5 additional days for delivery.

Cooper Law’s obligation is fulfilled upon delivery of completed documents to the postal carrier. Delivery timelines for mailed kits are subject to carrier conditions outside of Cooper Law’s control. Clients with time-sensitive deadlines are strongly encouraged to choose electronic delivery.

🤝

In-person signing

Louisville-area clients are welcome to schedule an in-person signing at the Cooper Law office. For clients who are hospitalized or unable to travel, Allison is available to visit local Louisville-area hospitals by arrangement.

In-person signings are available by appointment and subject to scheduling availability. Hospital visits are limited to the Louisville area. Please mention your preference in your intake form.
