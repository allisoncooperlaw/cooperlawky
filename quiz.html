Do you want your estate to skip probate?

Probate is the court process that distributes your assets after death. It’s public, takes 6–24 months, and typically costs 2–4% of the estate. A trust avoids it entirely.

Are you planning as an individual or a couple?

Couples share some documents but each person needs their own will, POA, and directives.

How do you and your spouse hold assets?

This determines which trust structure makes sense. Don’t worry if you’re not sure — pick your best guess and we’ll confirm during the planning process.
