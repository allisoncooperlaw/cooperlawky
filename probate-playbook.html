Who this book is for

If your family situation looks like this, the Probate Playbook is built for you:

  • The heirs agree on what should happen
  • The will is clear (or there’s no will but the heirs are obvious)
  • The assets are straightforward — a house, accounts, maybe a vehicle
  • There are no significant creditor disputes
  • Nobody’s contesting the will
  • You’re willing to read carefully and follow steps

If any of these apply, you probably want an attorney instead:

  • Heirs are fighting or someone is threatening to contest
  • A business needs to be valued, sold, or wound down
  • There’s real estate in multiple states
  • Significant debt or tax issues
  • A trust is involved and needs to be administered
  • You don’t have time to read and follow steps

The book will help you figure out which category you’re in before you commit to a DIY approach.
